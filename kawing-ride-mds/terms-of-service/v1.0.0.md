# Terms of Service

Last updated: February 17, 2026.

These Terms of Service governed the first public release of Kawing Ride's policy framework. They remain available for transparency so users can see the operating rules that applied before the later trust and governance refinements in `v1.1.0`.

## Service Scope

Kawing Ride is a coordination platform for private community ride requests. The platform helps customers and riders connect inside trusted groups and provides tools for request handling, negotiation, and basic accountability.

Kawing Ride is not the direct provider of transportation services and does not employ riders as drivers for individual trips. The ride arrangement remains between the users involved in that request.

## Eligibility and Account Integrity

Users must provide truthful information when requesting access, creating an account, or onboarding into a trusted role.

You may not impersonate another person, create misleading accounts, or keep using Kawing Ride after access has been revoked unless Kawing Ride expressly restores that access.

## Requests, Responses, and Agreement

Customers may create a request and riders may respond through the platform. A ride is not considered final until the parties clearly agree on the relevant terms.

Customers should provide accurate trip details. Riders should only respond if they can genuinely serve the trip on the terms being discussed.

## Pricing and Payment

Kawing Ride may provide community pricing guidance or reference information to help users negotiate more fairly. Those references are informational and do not replace the need for actual agreement between customer and rider.

Unless Kawing Ride later launches its own payment feature, payment remains the responsibility of the users involved in the ride.

## Acceptable Use

Users may not use Kawing Ride to:

* commit fraud or impersonation;
* harass or threaten another person;
* share private information outside the intended workflow;
* evade community restrictions or moderation; or
* coordinate illegal or unsafe activity.

## Enforcement

Kawing Ride may issue warnings, restrict access, suspend accounts, or permanently remove users when needed to protect the platform or community trust.

Not every violation will receive a prior warning.

## Liability and Availability

Kawing Ride is provided on an "as is" and "as available" basis to the extent permitted by law.

Kawing Ride does not guarantee uninterrupted service, successful trip completion, or the truthfulness of every user on the platform.
