# Data Retention Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026.

This archived version reflects Kawing Ride's initial retention approach before later refinements to category-specific timelines.

## Retention Approach

Kawing Ride keeps records for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, support trust and safety reviews, investigate abuse, and comply with legal or audit obligations where applicable.

## General Categories

The platform may retain the following categories of records:

* customer profile and request history;
* rider onboarding and verification files;
* admin access and governance records;
* security and audit logs; and
* reports, disputes, and enforcement notes.

## Typical Windows

In the initial policy version, Kawing Ride generally expected to retain active operational records while an account or role remained active and for a reasonable period afterward where needed for safety, dispute review, or platform integrity.

More detailed windows were reserved for later policy updates as the product matured.

## Exceptions

Records may be kept longer when required for disputes, abuse investigations, legal requests, or the protection of users and communities.

## Deletion Requests

Account closure or deletion requests do not guarantee immediate removal of every record. Some information may still be retained where there is a legitimate security, legal, or review-related reason to keep it.
