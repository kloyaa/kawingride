# Rider Standards and Responsibilities

Last updated: April 10, 2026.

Riders are central to Kawing Ride's trust model. The platform is designed so customers do not have to do detective work before every ride, which means riders are expected to meet a higher standard of truthfulness, readiness, professionalism, and accountability.

These standards apply in addition to the [Terms of Service](/terms), the [Community Guidelines](/community-guidelines), and any community-specific onboarding conditions approved by Kawing Ride or the relevant admins.

## Truthful Onboarding and Ongoing Accuracy

Riders must provide accurate onboarding information and keep material details current. That includes identity details, vehicle information, plate number, legal eligibility, contact information, and any other records reasonably required for trusted participation.

Do not submit forged, altered, expired, borrowed, or misleading onboarding material. If a required document changes or a vehicle is no longer appropriate for service, update the information before continuing to accept requests.

## Legal and Operational Readiness

Riders are responsible for complying with the laws, licensing rules, permit requirements, insurance expectations, and vehicle obligations that apply to their activity. Kawing Ride does not assume those responsibilities for you.

A rider should not accept or pursue a request if the rider is not actually ready to perform the trip safely, lawfully, and on the agreed terms.

## Professional Responses and Pricing Conduct

Kawing Ride is designed to make negotiation feel structured rather than chaotic. Riders should respond clearly, respectfully, and in good faith.

* State price, timing, and relevant conditions honestly.
* Avoid bait-and-switch tactics or surprise charges after a negotiation appears settled.
* Use community pricing guidance and fuel context as reference points, not as excuses for manipulation.
* Respect the customer's right to compare options before making a decision.

Interest in a request does not guarantee selection. Riders must not harass customers for choosing another response.

## Privacy and Contact Boundaries

Customers may rely on Kawing Ride because it reveals personal information progressively. Riders must respect that design.

Do not pressure customers to reveal private details outside the intended workflow, and do not reuse customer data for unrelated marketing, solicitation, or off-platform targeting.

Any contact information or trip details received through Kawing Ride may be used only for legitimate ride coordination, follow-up directly related to that trip, or lawful safety reporting.

## Safety and Community Trust

Riders should act in ways that strengthen confidence in the community, not just complete a single trip.

* Communicate delays or changes promptly.
* Avoid unsafe driving or reckless behavior.
* Treat customers, admins, and moderators with respect.
* Cooperate with legitimate verification, review, and investigation requests.
* Report serious incidents, scams, or identity misuse that could affect others in the community.

Safety features, verification, and trust signals help reduce uncertainty, but they do not remove the rider's responsibility to use sound judgment during every trip.

## No Circumvention or Reputation Abuse

Riders may not use Kawing Ride to collect customers and then deliberately move coordination off-platform in order to avoid review, records, pricing scrutiny, moderation, or accountability for misconduct that started on the platform.

Riders also may not retaliate against customers for complaints, pressure admins for special treatment, or manipulate community trust by using alternate accounts, proxies, or borrowed identities.

## Enforcement

Violations of these standards may lead to onboarding rejection, temporary limits, removal from requests, suspension, permanent account removal, role revocation, or referral to community leadership or relevant authorities where appropriate.

Where rider conduct creates a meaningful risk to customer safety, privacy, or trust in the community, Kawing Ride may act quickly and without prior warning.
