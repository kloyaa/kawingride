# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026.

Kawing Ride is built around a simple promise: ride coordination for trusted communities should feel more private, more structured, and more accountable than broad public posting. This Privacy Policy explains how Kawing Ride collects, uses, shares, protects, and retains personal data in support of that mission.

This policy applies to website visitors, customers, riders, admins, moderators, and other users who interact with Kawing Ride products, onboarding flows, support channels, or policy processes.

## Scope of This Policy

Kawing Ride may process personal data through the public website, future mobile or web application experiences, onboarding and verification workflows, community administration tools, safety and trust systems, and incident review processes.

This policy should be read together with the [Terms of Service](/terms), the [Data Retention Policy](/data-retention), and the [Reporting and Appeals Process](/reporting-and-appeals).

## Data We May Collect

Kawing Ride collects different categories of data depending on your role and how you use the service.

### Account and Profile Information

We may collect your name, username, mobile number, community affiliation, login information, and other profile details needed to create or manage access.

For customer onboarding, Kawing Ride aims to keep the process lightweight where possible. For rider and admin onboarding, more detailed identification or role-verification information may be required because those roles carry greater trust and governance responsibilities.

### Ride Request and Negotiation Data

We may collect trip details, schedules, pickup and drop-off information, negotiation history, selected responses, cancellations, confirmations, and related ride lifecycle activity.

This information helps the platform preserve context, reduce disputes, and support a clearer operating record for communities that already coordinate rides locally.

### Rider, Admin, and Moderator Verification Data

Where applicable, Kawing Ride may collect protected onboarding records such as identification details, vehicle information, plate number, selfie or liveness checks, background details, admin authorization details, and other records reasonably needed to verify that a person can hold a trusted role in the community.

Access to these records is restricted and governed by role-based controls, internal review needs, and the [Admin and Moderator Standards](/admin-guidelines).

### Device, Security, and Trust Data

We may automatically collect technical and security-related data such as device identifiers, browser information, IP address, approximate location signals, connection logs, audit trails, and device fingerprinting signals used to strengthen fraud prevention, protect account access, and support moderation review.

Kawing Ride uses these signals to improve trust, not to turn the product into a public surveillance system. The goal is to reduce abuse while keeping the coordination experience privacy-aware.

### Support, Reporting, and Appeal Records

If you contact Kawing Ride, submit an incident report, request access, or appeal an enforcement decision, we may collect the content of those submissions, supporting evidence, communications history, and review notes.

## How We Use Personal Data

Kawing Ride may use personal data to:

* Provide, secure, maintain, and improve the platform.
* Create and manage accounts, community access, and role assignments.
* Power ride coordination, negotiation, and confirmation flows.
* Support trust, fraud prevention, safety review, and moderation decisions.
* Protect users, communities, and the service against abuse or unauthorized access.
* Send operational, verification, security, and policy-related communications.
* Respond to support questions, reports, disputes, legal requests, or appeals.
* Meet recordkeeping, legal, regulatory, audit, or compliance obligations where applicable.

## How Data Is Shared

Kawing Ride does not sell personal data.

Kawing Ride may share personal data only as reasonably needed for the service to operate, including in the following situations:

* With other users when needed to complete the ride lifecycle or support a confirmed booking.
* With authorized admins or moderators when they need access to protected records, trust signals, or incident details to carry out legitimate platform duties.
* With service providers that help Kawing Ride operate infrastructure such as hosting, communications, storage, analytics, verification, and security tools.
* With legal authorities, regulators, courts, or law enforcement when Kawing Ride is required to do so or reasonably believes disclosure is necessary to protect rights, safety, or the integrity of the platform.
* In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, or similar business transaction, subject to appropriate confidentiality and lawful handling requirements.

Kawing Ride aims to reveal personal information progressively rather than broadly. For example, certain contact details may remain hidden during early negotiation and be shared only when the workflow or user settings allow it.

## Security and Access Controls

Kawing Ride uses administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal data against unauthorized access, misuse, disclosure, alteration, and loss.

These safeguards may include encryption, audit logging, role-based access restrictions, controlled onboarding review, session protection, device-based trust checks, and internal access limitations for sensitive records.

No system can promise absolute security. Users also play a role by protecting their credentials, reporting suspicious activity quickly, and using the platform responsibly.

## Retention and Deletion

Kawing Ride keeps personal data only for as long as it is reasonably needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer period is required or justified by law, safety, fraud prevention, active disputes, or platform integrity needs.

More detail about record categories and retention windows is available in the [Data Retention Policy](/data-retention).

## Your Choices and Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights relating to your personal data, including the right to request access, correction, objection, erasure or blocking, data portability, or review of how your information is handled.

Kawing Ride may ask you to verify your identity before acting on a request. Some requests may be limited where records must be kept for safety, fraud prevention, legal compliance, audit trails, active disputes, or the protection of other users and communities.

If you believe your data privacy rights have been violated, you may also have the right to raise a concern with the relevant supervisory authority, including the National Privacy Commission in the Philippines where applicable.

## Privacy Contact

For privacy-related questions, data rights requests, or concerns about how Kawing Ride handles personal data, contact [privacy@kawingride.com](mailto:privacy@kawingride.com).

## Children and Restricted Use

Kawing Ride is not intended for use by people who are not legally permitted to form a binding agreement or who are prohibited from participating in the relevant community or service. If Kawing Ride learns that personal data was collected in a manner that should not have occurred, the platform may restrict access and take appropriate remedial steps.

## Cross-Border Processing

Kawing Ride may use infrastructure or service providers that process data in jurisdictions other than the one where a user is located. When this happens, Kawing Ride expects those providers to handle data under appropriate contractual, security, and confidentiality controls.

## Policy Updates

Kawing Ride may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in product features, operational practices, applicable requirements, or security controls. When updates are posted, the new version becomes effective on the stated date unless otherwise noted.

Continued use of Kawing Ride after the effective date means you accept the updated policy.
