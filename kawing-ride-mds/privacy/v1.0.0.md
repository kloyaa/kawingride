# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026.

This archived version of the Privacy Policy reflects Kawing Ride's initial privacy commitments for the first public policy release.

## What We Collect

Kawing Ride may collect account details, contact information, ride request information, rider onboarding materials, and technical logs needed to operate the service responsibly.

For trusted roles such as riders and admins, Kawing Ride may collect more detailed information than it collects from lightweight customer onboarding.

## How We Use Data

Kawing Ride may use personal data to:

* operate the platform;
* manage access and trusted roles;
* support ride coordination and negotiation;
* investigate fraud or abuse; and
* communicate operational or security-related notices.

## Sharing

Kawing Ride does not sell personal data.

Information may be shared with other users when necessary to coordinate a ride, with authorized admins for legitimate governance work, with service providers supporting infrastructure, or with authorities when legally required.

## Security

Kawing Ride uses reasonable safeguards intended to protect personal data, including role-based access controls and system security measures appropriate to the service.

No method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## Retention

Kawing Ride keeps data for as long as reasonably needed to operate the platform, support reviews, respond to disputes, and comply with applicable obligations.

Specific retention periods may be updated in later policy versions.

## User Requests

Users may request access to or correction of their personal data, subject to verification and lawful limitations.

Some records may still be kept after account closure where needed for platform security, dispute handling, or compliance.
