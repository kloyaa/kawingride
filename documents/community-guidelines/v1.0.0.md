# Community Guidelines

Last updated: February 17, 2026.

This archived version captures the original community behavior expectations for Kawing Ride's first public framework.

## Respect and Honesty

Treat other users with respect and provide truthful information when requesting rides, responding as a rider, or participating in any community workflow.

Threats, harassment, discrimination, and deceptive behavior are not allowed.

## Privacy

Do not expose another person's ride details, phone number, or protected onboarding records outside the intended community process unless disclosure is genuinely necessary for safety or required by law.

## Good-Faith Negotiation

Use the platform's negotiation flow honestly.

* Do not spam requests.
* Do not mislead users about price or availability.
* Do not pressure another user into sharing private information too early.

## Safety

Do not use Kawing Ride for unsafe, illegal, or coercive activity.

If a matter is urgent or dangerous, contact the appropriate authorities rather than relying on the platform alone.

## Reporting

Users should report scams, abuse, unsafe conduct, or serious privacy misuse in good faith.

False or retaliatory reporting may itself lead to enforcement action.
