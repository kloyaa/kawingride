# Admin and Moderator Standards

Last updated: February 17, 2026.

This archived version preserves the first published governance standards for admins and moderators.

## Legitimate Use of Access

Admins and moderators may use protected access only for valid platform and community purposes such as onboarding review, incident handling, access approvals, and moderation.

Protected records are not for personal curiosity or informal sharing.

## Confidentiality

Sensitive onboarding and review information must be handled carefully.

* Do not disclose protected records publicly.
* Do not use private information to embarrass or pressure a user.
* Report suspected misuse of sensitive access quickly.

## Fair Decision-Making

Governance decisions should be based on relevant facts and community protection, not favoritism or personal conflict.

Where possible, reviewers should avoid handling matters where they have a direct conflict of interest.

## Security

Admin and moderator credentials must be protected. Shared logins and careless handling of access details are inconsistent with this role.

## Loss of Privileges

Kawing Ride may revoke governance access when an admin or moderator misuses authority, exposes protected data, or otherwise undermines trust in the community.
