# Rider Standards and Responsibilities

Last updated: February 17, 2026.

This archived version reflects the first rider standards published as part of the Kawing Ride trust model.

## Accurate Onboarding

Riders must provide truthful onboarding details and keep important records current.

You may not use forged, borrowed, or misleading documents to gain or keep rider access.

## Lawful and Ready Service

Riders are responsible for meeting the legal and practical requirements that apply to their activity, vehicle, and service readiness.

Do not accept requests you cannot safely or lawfully complete.

## Honest Negotiation

Respond to customer requests clearly and in good faith.

* State price and timing honestly.
* Avoid surprise charges.
* Respect the customer's final decision.

## Privacy and Conduct

Use customer data only for legitimate ride coordination and related safety or support matters.

Do not harass customers, retaliate over complaints, or move coordination off-platform in order to avoid accountability for misconduct.

## Enforcement

Rider violations may lead to onboarding rejection, request limits, suspension, or permanent removal where needed to protect community trust.
